Patch Notes 14.22

General Changes

Patch 14.22 brings a variety of changes to the game, including updates to champions, items, and overall balance adjustments. Riot continues to refine gameplay and provide better experiences for all types of players.

Champion Updates

Akali

Akali sees a slight buff this patch to her ability to poke and sustain in the laning phase. Her base health has been increased, and the cooldown of her ultimate has been reduced for faster engages.

  • Base Health: Increased from 570 to 600
  • Ultimate - Perfect Execution: Cooldown reduced from 120/100/80 seconds to 100/90/80 seconds

Jinx

Jinx has been slightly nerfed to bring her damage output in line with other ADCs. Her attack speed scaling has been reduced, making her less oppressive in the early game.

  • Attack Speed Scaling: Decreased from 0.8 to 0.7 per level
  • Q - Switcheroo! Attack Speed bonus reduced from 50/75/100/125% to 50/70/90/110%

Zed

Zed receives some quality-of-life adjustments to his energy regeneration and laning phase damage. His early clear potential is improved while keeping his mid- and late-game balanced.

  • Energy Regeneration: Increased from 45% to 50%
  • E - Shadow Slash: Damage increased from 70/110/150/190/230 to 80/120/160/200/240

Item Adjustments

Galeforce

Galeforce is being reworked to increase its utility in late-game skirmishes. The item’s passive now scales with attack speed and grants bonus movement speed after activating its dash ability.

  • Active - Cloudburst: New effect – grants bonus movement speed for 1.5 seconds after dashing, increasing with attack speed.
  • Cost: Increased from 3200 gold to 3300 gold

Sunfire Aegis

Sunfire Aegis is getting buffed to give more damage to tanky champions. The passive damage has been increased, rewarding tank players more for engaging in prolonged fights.

  • Passive - Immolate: Damage increased from 35 to 45
  • Cost: Decreased from 3000 gold to 2800 gold

Bug Fixes and Miscellaneous Updates

Bug Fixes

Several minor bugs have been fixed in Patch 14.22, including visual bugs, some incorrect tooltips, and other issues that affected gameplay for specific champions and items.

  • Fixed an issue where Sett’s “The Showstopper” would occasionally not properly knock up enemies in certain areas.
  • Fixed a bug where Soraka’s W would not heal the full amount when cast in the jungle.

Miscellaneous Updates

A new feature has been introduced in Patch 14.22 allowing players to save custom loadouts for champions. This makes it easier to experiment with new builds and optimize setups for specific matchups.

Conclusion

Patch 14.22 focuses on minor balance adjustments that affect the meta both in terms of champion power and item efficiency. Whether you’re playing your favorite marksman or bruiser, this patch brings tweaks to help refine the gameplay experience. Riot Games continues to listen to community feedback, so keep an eye out for future changes as the season progresses.
