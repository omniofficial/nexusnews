Patch Notes 14.21: New Changes and Updates

Champion Adjustments

Patch 14.21 brings a range of changes to some of the most popular champions in the game. Riot Games continues to fine-tune balance with these adjustments, making sure the meta remains dynamic and diverse.

  • Ahri: Ahri receives a slight buff to her charm duration and overall mobility. Her ability to maneuver around team fights and influence the battlefield will make her more impactful in the late game.
  • Lee Sin: Lee Sin sees a slight reduction in damage on his Q ability, but a slight increase in sustain through his passive. This change aims to make him more durable in the early stages of the game.
  • Jhin: Jhin's ultimate now has a lower cooldown, allowing him to play more aggressively and apply more pressure during team fights. His damage scaling also improves to increase his relevance late game.

Item Updates

Several items have been updated to balance their power, particularly in terms of sustain and overall effectiveness in various game modes.

  • Divine Sunderer: The healing provided from this item has been reduced in order to curb its overuse in bruiser builds, especially in the early game where it provided excessive sustain.
  • Ravenous Hydra: The lifesteal on this item has been increased slightly, offering stronger sustain for AD champions who focus on scaling into the late game.

System Changes

The patch also introduces changes to various systems to enhance gameplay and improve quality of life for players.

  • Minion Changes: A small change has been made to how minions interact with champions, providing more consistent experience gains for solo laners.
  • Jungle Changes: The jungle has seen an increase in XP rewards for camps in the early game, allowing junglers to ramp up quicker and support their teams more effectively in the early skirmishes.

Bug Fixes

As always, Riot Games has included a list of bug fixes to improve the overall experience. Some key fixes include:

  • Graves: Fixed an issue where Graves' Q ability was incorrectly dealing reduced damage to turrets.
  • Miss Fortune: Resolved an issue where Miss Fortune's ult would not properly scale with her attack damage in some cases.
  • Katarina: Fixed a bug where Katarina's W ability was not properly resetting cooldowns after scoring a kill.

Upcoming Changes

Patch 14.21 is just the beginning, with Riot Games teasing future changes in the upcoming patches. Expect more balance tweaks, new champion releases, and exciting updates for the coming months.

Conclusion

Patch 14.21 continues the trend of making the game more dynamic and enjoyable for players of all levels. With numerous champion adjustments, item changes, and bug fixes, it is shaping up to be an exciting patch. Stay tuned for more updates in future patches as Riot Games continues to refine the League of Legends experience.
