Sejuani: Winter's Wrath

Champion Overview

Sejuani, the Winter’s Wrath, is a powerful tank and crowd control champion in League of Legends. As a jungler, Sejuani excels at disrupting enemies and creating opportunities for her team to secure objectives and team fights. With her tough resilience, Sejuani can control the battlefield and create chaos in enemy lines. Whether in the jungle or as a tank in team fights, Sejuani is a champion who thrives on crowd control and disruption.

Abilities Overview

Sejuani’s abilities are focused on controlling the enemy and providing utility for her team. Here’s a breakdown of her abilities:

  • Passive - Fury of the North: Sejuani’s passive increases her resistance to crowd control effects, while also granting bonus resistances when near allies, making her more durable in team fights.
  • Q - Arctic Assault: Sejuani charges forward in a targeted direction, knocking aside and stunning any enemy she collides with. This ability is a great tool for both initiation and disengagement.
  • W - Winter's Wrath: Sejuani swings her flail in a wide area, dealing damage to enemies and applying a slow effect. This ability helps in clearing camps quickly and controlling multiple enemies in a fight.
  • E - Permafrost: Sejuani slows down enemies and applies a damaging debuff. This ability synergizes well with her other abilities and helps provide additional crowd control to enemies who are hit by her Q and W.
  • R - Glacial Prison: Sejuani hurls a massive ice ball, stunning and damaging enemies it hits. This ultimate is one of the most effective initiation tools in the game, capable of turning the tide of a team fight when timed correctly.

Playstyle and Tips

Sejuani is a tanky jungler who thrives in team fights. Here are a few tips to help you master Sejuani:

  • Initiate Fights: Sejuani’s Q and R are excellent tools for initiating team fights. Look for an opportunity to use Glacial Prison on key enemy targets, like squishy carries, and follow up with Winter’s Wrath for extra crowd control.
  • Engage With Arctic Assault: Sejuani’s Q is great for quickly closing the gap on enemies. Use it to either engage or escape, and make sure to hit multiple enemies to maximize its effectiveness.
  • Manage Permafrost: Make sure you land your E on enemies already hit by your other abilities. This will maximize the damage output and provide additional crowd control when the enemy is most vulnerable.
  • Tank and Protect Your Team: Sejuani can absorb a lot of damage and protect squishy allies. Position yourself well to soak up damage and initiate on key targets during team fights.

Sejuani’s Role in the Meta

Sejuani is a strong pick in the jungle role due to her crowd control, tankiness, and high engage potential. She is particularly effective against squishier enemies and carries who rely on mobility to escape. Her ultimate, Glacial Prison, can often turn the tide of a team fight, and with her other abilities providing solid crowd control, Sejuani remains a top-tier pick in competitive play. She is particularly popular in coordinated team compositions where she can engage fights and soak damage for her allies.

Skin Collection

Sejuani’s fierce, battle-ready design has inspired several skins over the years. Some of her most popular skins include:

  • Winter Wonder Sejuani: A festive, snow-themed skin that gives Sejuani a cheerful and bright appearance, complete with icy effects on her abilities.
  • Steel Dragon Sejuani: A powerful and intimidating skin that gives Sejuani a dragon-inspired look, with fiery effects and a more menacing presence.
  • Battle Queen Sejuani: A regal skin where Sejuani is dressed as a queen of battle, wielding an elegant yet deadly appearance.
  • Beast Hunter Sejuani: This skin transforms Sejuani into a savage beast hunter, with a rough, primal look and animalistic effects on her abilities.

Lore

Sejuani is the brutal and fierce ruler of the Freljord’s Winter’s Claw tribe. She is a warrior of unrivaled skill and courage, leading her people with an iron fist to claim what is rightfully theirs. Sejuani rides into battle on her mighty warboar, Bristle, and her relentless pursuit of victory is guided by a single purpose: to ensure the survival of her tribe in the harsh, unforgiving lands of Freljord. As the Winter’s Wrath, Sejuani commands respect and fear, and she will stop at nothing to see her enemies crushed beneath the weight of her will.

Conclusion

Sejuani is a powerful tank jungler with immense crowd control abilities, capable of disrupting enemies and protecting her team. Her role in both initiating fights and absorbing damage makes her a pivotal champion in the meta, and she excels when played in a coordinated team. If you enjoy playing tanky junglers who thrive in team fights and offer utility through crowd control, Sejuani is an excellent choice for your champion pool.
